Higher National Diploma in Information Technology - (HNDIT)
උසස් ජාතික තොරතුරු තාක්ෂණ ඩිප්ලෝමා පාඨමාලාව





The Higher National Diploma in Information Technology (HNDIT) program at the Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced Technological Education (SLIATE) was developed and commenced in the year 2000 with the objective of producing the middle level IT professional required for the new millennium.


Course Duration: 2 ½ years
